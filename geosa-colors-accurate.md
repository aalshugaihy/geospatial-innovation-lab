# GEOSA Accurate Color Palette Analysis

Based on careful observation of the GEOSA website screenshot:

## Primary Colors

### Main Green (Primary Brand Color)
- **Dark Teal/Turquoise Green**: The primary color used in the header, buttons, and accents
- Appears to be around: `#00A99D` or `#00B5A5` (Turquoise/Teal)
- This is a **cyan-teal green**, NOT forest green

### Background
- **White**: Clean white background `#FFFFFF`
- **Light Gray**: Very light gray for sections `#F8F9FA` or similar

### Text Colors
- **Dark Gray/Black**: Primary text color `#1A1A1A` or `#2D2D2D`
- **Medium Gray**: Secondary text `#6B6B6B` or `#757575`

### Accent Colors
- **Bright Cyan/Turquoise**: `#00BCD4` or similar - used for CTAs and highlights
- **Light Teal Background**: `#E0F7F5` or similar - for card backgrounds

## Correct Color Scheme for Innovation Lab

The GEOSA website uses:
1. **Primary**: Teal/Turquoise Cyan (#00A99D, #00B5A5, #00BCD4 range)
2. **Secondary**: White and very light grays
3. **Text**: Dark grays and blacks
4. **Accents**: Bright cyan for interactive elements

## Key Observations
- The green is **NOT forest green** (#1B5E20) - it's much lighter and more cyan/teal
- The overall feel is **modern, clean, and tech-forward**
- Heavy use of white space
- Teal/cyan is the dominant brand color throughout

## Recommended Updates
Replace the current forest green (#1B5E20) with teal/cyan (#00A99D or #00BCD4)
