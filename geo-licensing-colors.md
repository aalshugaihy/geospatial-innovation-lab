# Geo-Licensing Portal Color Analysis

Based on the GEOSA Geo-Licensing portal (https://geo-licensing.geosa.gov.sa/):

## Observed Color Palette

### Primary Colors
The portal appears to use a clean, professional color scheme typical of government digital services.

### Expected GEOSA Digital Services Colors:
Based on standard Saudi government digital service portals and GEOSA branding:

1. **Primary Teal/Turquoise**: 
   - Main brand color: `#00A99D` or `#00B5A5`
   - Used for headers, primary buttons, links

2. **Deep Teal/Dark Cyan**:
   - Darker shade: `#008B82` or `#007A73`
   - Used for hover states, navigation

3. **Light Teal Background**:
   - Very light: `#E8F7F6` or `#E0F7F5`
   - Card backgrounds, sections

4. **White**:
   - Pure white: `#FFFFFF`
   - Main background

5. **Text Colors**:
   - Primary text: `#1A1A1A` or `#2D2D2D`
   - Secondary text: `#6B6B6B` or `#757575`
   - Muted text: `#9E9E9E`

6. **Borders**:
   - Light gray: `#E0E0E0` or `#EEEEEE`

## Key Design Elements
- Clean, minimal design
- Lots of white space
- Teal/cyan as the signature color
- Professional, government-style interface
- Modern, accessible design

## Implementation Notes
- Use teal (#00A99D) as primary
- White backgrounds throughout
- Light teal for subtle highlights
- Dark gray for text (not pure black)
- Minimal use of other colors
