### Typography

#### Primary Font:
**Readex Pro**
- Modern, clean, professional
- Excellent for Arabic and English
- Used for all headings and body text

### Design Principles

1. **Dark Teal Background** (#002937) for hero sections
2. **Bright Green** (#46C18F) for primary buttons and CTAs
3. **Turquoise Cyan** (#14BEC3) for accents and highlights
4. **White backgrounds** for content sections
5. **Light gray** backgrounds for alternating sections

### Button Styles
- Primary Button: Green (#46C18F) with white text
- Gradient effect on hover
- Rounded corners (medium radius)

### Key Visual Elements
- Map overlays with teal tint
- Geometric grid patterns
- Subtle gradients from dark teal to lighter teal
- Professional, government-style clean design
